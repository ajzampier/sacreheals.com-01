 Important: Avoid the Disadvantages of Chargebacks 

While chargebacks through your bank or credit card company may seem like a quick solution, they often cause unnecessary delays and complications. Here's why we recommend requesting a refund directly with us:

- Faster Resolution:

Chargebacks can take weeks or even months to resolve, while refunds via our process are typically handled within 24 hours.

- Avoid Additional Fees:

Banks may impose extra fees, and you might need to provide extensive documentation, leading to unnecessary frustration.

- Protect Your Relationship with Us:

Chargebacks can negatively impact your customer account and future access to exclusive offers or benefits reserved for loyal clients.

By choosing our refund process, you’ll save time, avoid hassle, and maintain a positive relationship with our company.
